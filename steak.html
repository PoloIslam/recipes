Rib-Eye Steak

Description:

A Rib Eye steak is a classical cut that is known to be tender and full of flavor. It's rich marbling melts together with the meat while cooking giving it it's iconic flavor.

Ingredients:

  • (4) 1/2 pound (oz) of Rib-eye Steak
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt and peper each
  • 1 and 3/4 Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • Vegtable oil for brushing grill

Directions:

  1. Season steaks with garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper.
  2. Place Worcestershire sauce and brown sugar in large resealable bag, shake and mix.
  3. Pour 1/2 of the marinade into another large resealable bag and place 2 steaks in each bag to coat. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat outdoor grill to high-heat and lightly oil the grate
  5. Drain marinade from the bags into a small saucepan
  6. Boil and cook the marinade for 3-4 minutes then set aside.
  7. Grill Steaks on preheated grlil to preferred doneness, roughly 7 minutes per side.
  8. Now baste the boiled marinade in the final 5 miinutes of cook time on the steaks. (Thermometer should read 140F (60C) in the center of the steak for medium doneness)
  9. Enjoy!
