Tandoori Chicken

Description:

Tandoori chicken is a flavorful, spicy dish of chicken that's marinated in yogurt and spices. This popular dish brings some spice and flavor to anyones food pallete!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Pounds of Chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/4 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons of finely chopped cilantro
  • 1 medium lemon, cut into wedges

Directions:

  1. Remove and discard skin from chicken and cut slits into meat to place in a shallow dish.
  2. Season chicken on both sides with lemon juice and salt and let sit for 20 minutes.
  3. Mix yogurt, onion, garlic, garam masala, ginger, and cayenne pepper together until smooth.
  4. Spread yogurt mixture over chicken, cover and refigerate for 6 to 24 hours. (The longer the better)
  5. When ready to cook, preheat grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil grate.
  6. Remove chicken from marinade and discard remaining marinade.
  7. Cook Chicken on preheated grill until no longer pink and juices run clear. Thermometer inserted near bone should read 165F.(74C)
  8. Garnish with cilantro and lemon wedges.
  9. Enjoy!
